Features: Assignment 1 - Group Wiki Project

The focus of Assignment 1 was to work in a small team and create an authentic online learning module, demonstrating a range of digital and media affordances of the web environment. The chosen topic related to digital citizenship in an education context. This module was created using a wiki (Wikispaces) and required a high level of group communication, collaboration and co-creation skills as well as some technical skills.

Strategies: Collaborative group projects, Creating a shared resource
