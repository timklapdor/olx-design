Overview

In essence what is the exemplar & how does it work? What did you ask the learners to do? How did they interact? What are the key points and critical factors? Explain it simply and clearly. ?

Motivation

What motivated the initiative? What problem were you trying to address, or what improvement were you trying to make? What was the context of the initiative? You might like to illustrate this by including information about class size, mode of study, year, post or under grad, courses which the subject is included within.

Testimonials

What was the experience like for the Staff, Students or Professionals involved? What are some of the benefits and opportunities? How transferable is this to other contexts? What are some of the problems and risks? What kind of advice would you give to someone attempting this? Other reflections?

Demonstration

We’d like to capture a snapshot of what the initiative looks like and how it works. This might be an overview of the process involved but perhaps a video or screencast of how it was set up and functioned may be a better format.

Assessment

How is learning being assessed or linked to assessment? If their is no direct link - how does the initiative support students in their assessment tasks?

Literature & Resources

What Literature and Resources informed the initiative? Has there been any related scholarly publications that have been produced?

Q&A

In this section imagine some of the kinds of questions someone may ask of your initiative and provide some quick answers (remember we can hyperlink out to external sources). Why do this? What were the issues? Explain some of the benefits.

Terminology & Explanations

How To

Provide some recommendation on how others may go adopting or adapting this initiative. Provide some steps and tips for implementation. Include some information about time lines, hours needed to run, lessons learnt, and what disciplines it is likely to be useful for. What was needed to make this initiative successful (technologies, resources, access…)? (Include a “Where to Next” button to direct academics to specific Services)

Evaluation

If available summarise the evidence available to inform judgements about the effectiveness. Is it achieving its aim? Utilise SES evaluations, other student evaluations, informal feedback to establish what worked well? Were there any significant changes to the quality of students’ assessments?
